Share Without
Insecurity



State-of-the-art end-to-end
encryption (powered by the open
source Signal Protocol) keeps your
conversations secure. We can't read
your messages or listen to your calls,
and no one else can either. Privacy
isn’t an optional mode — it’s just the
way that Signal works. Every message,
every call, every time.

Make Privacy Stick


Share text, voice messages, photos, videos, GIFs and files for free.

and MMS fees.
